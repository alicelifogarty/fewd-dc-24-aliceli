About

California Coalition for Rural Housing (CCRH) is a statewide nonprofit oranization that works to ensure affordable housing opportunities for low income households in Caltifornia. Following their desire for a fun and informal look and feel, I designed a t-shirt, e-mail and print invitation, and donation card for their Rural West Internship Program graduation ceremony and party for 2016.
